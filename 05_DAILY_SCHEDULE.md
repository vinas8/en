# ⏱️ DAILY INTERLEAVED STUDY LOOP
Based on cognitive science, sessions should mix related skills to improve long-term retention. 

### Block 1: Active Recall Vocabulary (20 Min)
* Focus on high-frequency chunks (e.g., *I want to*, *Could you help me*).
* Prompt with Thai, require English production.

### Block 2: Comprehensible Input (20 Min)
* Listen to pop music (James Arthur's "Impossible", Jessie J's "Flashlight").
* Read lyrics while listening to absorb grammar naturally.

### Block 3: Speaking & Pronunciation (20 Min)
* Practice daily chunks aloud. 
* AI provides feedback on specific Thai hurdles (r/l, v/w, th sounds).

### Block 4: Spaced Repetition Review (20 Min)
* Test the hardest items from Day 1, Day 3, Day 7, etc.
* Focus on correcting translation errors (e.g., fixing *go to your country* to *When are you going back home?*).
