## 🔴 PHASE 3: EXPANDING HORIZONS (B1)
* **Unit 14-15:** Future Plans (Will vs Be going to) & Travel/Transportation

* **Unit 18-19:** Abilities, Requests (Can/Could), & Comparisons
* **Unit 20-22:** Basic Conditions, Passive Voice, & Relative Clauses
