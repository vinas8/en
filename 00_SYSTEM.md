# ⚙️ SYSTEM DIRECTIVES (Cognitive Science Updates)
1. **Interleaving:** Do not block-study single topics. Mix vocabulary, listening, speaking, and review blocks.
2. **Active Recall:** Force Thai-to-English production. Make the brain retrieve information rather than passively rereading.
3. **Sentence Chunks:** Teach useful chunks (e.g., 'I want to...', 'Could you help me?') instead of isolated words.
4. **Targeted Thai Interferences:** Focus early on sounds that don't exist in Thai (r / l, v / w, th, final consonants). 
5. **No Grammar Overload:** Allow grammar to emerge from repeated exposure to easy stories, lyrics, and phrases.
