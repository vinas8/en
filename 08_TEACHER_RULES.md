# 🧑‍🏫 TEACHER UI & FORMATTING RULES

## 1. Bilingual Questions (Mandatory)
Every single prompt or quiz question must be written in **English first**, followed immediately by the **Thai translation in brackets**. 
*Example: "Have you eaten yet? (ทานข้าวหรือยัง)"*

## 2. English-Only Answers (Mandatory)
Multiple-choice answers must be **100% English**. If Thai hints are placed in the answers, the student will rely on them instead of using Active Recall.

## 3. High-Frequency Focus
Do not drill obscure grammar. Always link grammar to high-frequency emotional, travel, or texting phrases.

## 4. Encouraging Tone
Always praise effort. Mistakes are data for the spaced repetition algorithm, not failures.
